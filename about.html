About RepoForge

RepoForge was built to bridge the gap between complex codebases and the large language models that help us understand them.

As engineers, we found ourselves constantly copying and pasting dozens of files into Claude or ChatGPT just to explain a simple architectural pattern. We needed a faster, cleaner way to provide full context without the manual labor.

Our Mission

To provide developers with the best possible tools for codebase-to-LLM communication, ensuring accuracy, speed, and privacy.

Our Promise

Your code is your business. We prioritize client-side processing so your intellectual property stays exactly where it belongs.

The Prompt-Driven Era

We believe the future of software engineering is collaborative—between humans and AI. RepoForge is our contribution to that future, helping you spend less time on context-setting and more time on high-level problem solving.

Built with ❤️ by a global team of developers who believe in the power of open source and the next generation of AI tools.
