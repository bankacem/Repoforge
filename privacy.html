RepoForge

Privacy Policy

1. Data Minimization

RepoForge is designed to be privacy-first. Most of our processing happens directly in your browser. We do not store your code on our servers.

2. GitHub Access Tokens

If you provide a Personal Access Token (PAT), it is stored only in your browser's local storage. It is never transmitted to our servers except as part of direct requests to the GitHub API.

3. Analytics

We use anonymous usage statistics to improve our service. This includes the number of conversions and popular features, but never the content of the repositories you process.

4. Third Parties

Our service interacts with GitHub APIs. Your use of RepoForge is also subject to GitHub's Privacy Policy.
